What is The Good Template?

The Good Template is a responsive, responsible web design framework designed in LESS. It attempts to lay out a strong text baseline— for everything. It wants to create sensible form stylesheets and simple sweet responsive grids for multiple screen sizes. It pays its taxes and has two strapping young children.

Features.

  • Awesome text baseline.
  • Stupendous responsive grid.
  • Grand form styling.
    1. Validation!
    2. Selects!
  • Strong HTML5 templates.
  • Crazy LESS mixins.
